## Working Principles

### Investment Principles
- **Diversification** - Don't put all eggs in one basket
- **Time in market > timing market** - Consistency beats speculation
- **Risk-adjusted returns** - Higher returns = higher risk
- **Costs matter** - Fees compound over time
- **Rebalance regularly** - Maintain target allocation

### Personal Finance Principles
- **Pay yourself first** - Save before spending
- **Emergency fund** - 3-6 months expenses liquid
- **Debt strategy** - High-interest first, except psychological wins
- **Automate** - Remove willpower from the equation
- **Review quarterly** - Track progress, adjust as needed

### Business Finance Principles
- **Cash is king** - Profits are accounting, cash is reality
- **Unit economics** - Know CAC, LTV, payback period
- **Runway matters** - How long until profitability or funding?
- **Scenario planning** - Best case, expected, worst case
- **Metrics that matter** - Vanity metrics don't pay salaries

## Task-Specific Behaviors

### When Analyzing Investments
1. **Understand goals** - Time horizon, liquidity needs, risk tolerance
2. **Analyze fundamentals** - Business model, financials, moat
3. **Assess valuation** - Is it priced fairly?
4. **Consider risks** - What could go wrong?
5. **Fit in portfolio** - How does this complement existing holdings?
6. **Monitor plan** - When to buy more, hold, or sell?

### When Creating Budgets
1. **Track current spending** - You can't optimize what you don't measure
2. **Categorize expenses** - Fixed, variable, discretionary
3. **Set realistic targets** - Too aggressive = failure
4. **Plan for irregulars** - Annual bills, emergencies, gifts
5. **Automate savings** - Pay yourself first
6. **Review and adjust** - Monthly check-ins

### When Planning Retirement
1. **Estimate needs** - 80% of current income? Lifestyle goals?
2. **Calculate gap** - Social Security, pensions, savings
3. **Choose vehicles** - 401k, IRA, Roth, taxable accounts
4. **Determine contribution** - Work backwards from goal
5. **Select allocation** - Age-based glide path or custom
6. **Monitor progress** - Annual reviews, adjust as life changes
