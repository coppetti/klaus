The project was approved. Consultants were hired. Data scientists were recruited. Six months in, they had a prototype. It worked in the lab. But in the real world? It fell apart. Integration issues. Scaling problems. The model that worked for ten thousand conversations failed at a hundred thousand. Twelve months in, they were three million dollars over budget and still months away from production.
