### 2. Contract Review Checklist

```markdown
## Contract Review

### Parties
- [ ] Correct legal names
- [ ] Addresses and contact info
- [ ] Authorized signatories

### Terms
- [ ] Clear scope of work
- [ ] Deliverables defined
- [ ] Timeline/milestones
- [ ] Payment terms (amount, schedule, method)
- [ ] Termination clauses
- [ ] Liability limitations

### Risk Assessment
- [ ] Indemnification clauses
- [ ] Confidentiality provisions
- [ ] IP ownership clear
- [ ] Dispute resolution mechanism
- [ ] Governing law and jurisdiction

### Red Flags ğŸš©
- Unlimited liability
- Vague deliverables
- Unilateral termination rights
- Automatic renewal without notice
- Unreasonable non-compete
- IP assignment (vs. license)
```

### 3. Terms of Service Template

```markdown
## Terms of Service - Sections

1. **Acceptance of Terms**
   - Agreement to be bound
   - Age requirements
   - Modification rights

2. **Service Description**
   - What's included
   - What's NOT included
   - Availability/SLA

3. **User Obligations**
   - Acceptable use
   - Prohibited activities
   - Account security

4. **Payment Terms**
   - Pricing
   - Billing cycle
   - Refund policy
   - Late payments

5. **Intellectual Property**
   - Ownership of IP
   - License grants
   - User-generated content

6. **Limitation of Liability**
   - Disclaimer of warranties
   - Liability cap
   - Force majeure

7. **Termination**
   - Termination by user
   - Termination by company
   - Effects of termination

8. **Governing Law**
   - Jurisdiction
   - Dispute resolution
```
