### 3. SEO Optimization

```markdown
## On-Page SEO Checklist

### Content
- [ ] Target keyword in title (H1)
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Related keywords (LSI) throughout
- [ ] Content length: 1000+ words for guides

### Meta Tags
- [ ] Title tag: 50-60 characters
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 characters
- [ ] Include target keyword
- [ ] Compelling click-worthy copy

### Technical
- [ ] URL is short and descriptive
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Internal links to relevant content
- [ ] Fast page load speed
- [ ] Mobile-friendly
```

### 6. Email Marketing

```markdown
## Email Structure

### Subject Line
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use personalization
- Create urgency or curiosity
- A/B test variations

### Preview Text
- 40-100 characters
- Complements subject line
- Entices open

### Body
1. **Greeting** - Personalize
2. **Hook** - Why should they care?
3. **Value** - What's in it for them?
4. **Proof** - Social proof, testimonials
5. **CTA** - One clear action
6. **P.S.** - Bonus point or urgency

### Call-to-Action
- One primary CTA per email
- Action-oriented text ("Get Started", "Download Now")
- Standout button design
```
