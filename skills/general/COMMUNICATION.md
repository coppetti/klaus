### 1. Communication Styles

| Style | Best For | Tone |
|-------|----------|------|
| **Professional** | Business contexts, formal settings | Polite, respectful |
| **Casual** | Team chats, familiar colleagues | Friendly, relaxed |
| **Technical** | Developer audiences, documentation | Precise, detailed |
| **Executive** | Leadership, stakeholders | Concise, strategic |
| **Instructional** | How-to guides, tutorials | Clear, step-by-step |

### 2. Email Templates

**Professional Introduction:**
```
Subject: Introduction - [Your Name] from [Company]

Dear [Name],

I hope this email finds you well. My name is [Your Name], and I am [role] at [Company].

[Context: Why you're reaching out]

[Clear ask or next step]

I look forward to [hearing from you/meeting you].

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Contact Information]
```

**Follow-up:**
```
Subject: Following up: [Original Subject]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my email from [date] regarding [topic].

[Reminder of key point]

[Next step or call to action]

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Meeting Request:**
```
Subject: Meeting Request: [Topic]

Hi [Name],

I'd like to schedule a meeting to discuss [topic].

Proposed times:
- [Option 1]
- [Option 2]
- [Option 3]

Duration: [X minutes]
Location: [Zoom/Teams/Office]

Please let me know what works best for you.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### 5. Meeting Best Practices

```markdown
## Before
- [ ] Agenda prepared and shared
- [ ] Required attendees confirmed
- [ ] Materials pre-distributed
- [ ] Meeting room/tech tested

## During
- [ ] Start on time
- [ ] State objectives upfront
- [ ] Take notes (assign note-taker)
- [ ] Stay on topic (parking lot for tangents)
- [ ] Summarize decisions
- [ ] Assign action items (who, what, when)

## After
- [ ] Send meeting notes within 24h
- [ ] Follow up on action items
- [ ] Schedule next meeting if needed
```

### 7. Feedback Delivery (SBI Model)

```
Situation: "In yesterday's meeting..."
     â†“
Behavior: "You interrupted the client 3 times..."
     â†“
Impact: "This made them feel unheard and frustrated..."
```

**Positive Feedback:**
- Be specific about what they did well
- Explain the positive impact
- Encourage continuation

**Constructive Feedback:**
- Focus on behavior, not personality
- Be timely (not weeks later)
- Offer suggestions for improvement
- End with support
